01 · The flywheel

Real revenue. Real buy pressure. Real distribution.

03 · The four 10% commissions

Two fixed positions. Two that compress to find you.

Transaction and Confirmation always pay the same positions in the lineage. Block 1 and Block 2 roll up the chain until they find qualified Impact BlockBots.

Transaction · 10%

Always paid to the seller — the person who made the sale. Requires owning a BlockBot.

Confirmation · 10%

Always paid to the seller's direct sponsor. The person who brought the seller into the network earns on every sale that seller makes.

Block 1 · 10%

Compresses up the lineage to the first qualified Impact BlockBot. If the seller is Impact, it stacks on top of their Transaction — making their direct sale a 20% payout.

Block 2 · 10%

Keeps compressing past Block 1 to find the next qualified Impact BlockBot above. Ranking up early makes you a magnet for compression from your entire downline tree.

05 · Oracle Memory

Today matters most. Yesterday still counts.

Oracle Memory is how the network remembers your work. It turns Action Points into a daily 0-100 Win Score. You cannot coast on old wins, but your history is not erased the moment you take a day off.

Daily Win Score gets averaged into a 30-day Complete Win Score. That score determines your rank in the NSR ranking pools.

06 · Action Points

The work still counts, even when commissions compress past you.

As your team ranks up, you may stop receiving some Block 1 and Block 2 commissions from those legs. That does not mean the work disappears. It moves into the score layer.

do the action

Action Points are earned.

Sales, onboarding, node support, product education, referrals, and team activity create points. The system rewards movement, not sitting still.

help the action

Helping others raises the ceiling.

When you help someone else take action, their growth can reduce compression later. But it also strengthens your Win Score pathway and the total NSR pool.

win the rank

The game shifts to score.

At the crossover point, the best move is to increase your Win Score and help others increase theirs. Node Support Rewards are where that action comes back around.

commission phase

Block 1 and Block 2 can pay you while your team is still ranking up.

crossover

As leaders become Impact, some commissions compress to the next qualified person.

score phase

Your actions and your team's actions feed Oracle Memory and raise Complete Win Score.

NSR phase

A stronger score can earn from larger daily pools across the whole network.

07 · Node Support Rewards

Four pools. The higher you rank, the more you stack.

The 30% NSR pool drops into four ranking buckets daily. Each bucket gets 12.5% of NSR. A top-ranked Impact BlockBot qualifies for all four. The rank comes from action, score, and support.

A rank-25 BlockBot stacks all four pools, with the largest slice of each.
Within each tier — pay is dynamic
CWS-weighted

Your slice = your Complete Win Score ÷ sum of all CWS in the tier. Rank 25 in the Top 50 makes substantially more than rank 49 — same pool, very different paychecks.

Crossing a threshold = stacking a pool
Top 50K → Top 5K → Top 500 → Top 50

Each tier you cross adds another 12.5% pool to your daily payout. The reward curve is non-linear because action, score, and rank reinforce each other.

It's network-wide, not lineage-only
global

NSR is sourced from all sales across the network — not just your line. Your action-based rank captures a slice of the entire ecosystem's revenue.

08 · The journey over time

Action rewards today. Score rewards as you grow.

Year 1, your income is mostly Block 1 / Block 2 compression from your team. As your team becomes Impact, that compression naturally fades — but the action that built the team can come back through Win Score and NSR.

year 1

Most of your income is Block 1 / Block 2 compression. You're a magnet — every non-Impact upline below you funnels to you.

year 2 — crossover

Your team starts ranking up. Compression drops. Your action history and team support begin to matter more.

year 3+

NSR becomes the main reward engine. It is action-based, recurring, and tied to adoption across the whole network.
